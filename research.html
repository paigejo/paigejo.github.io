Research

I specialize in spatial and spatiotemporal statistics, and am currently studying computational statistics, Bayesian statistics, INLA, small area estimation, and survey statistics. In particular, I am interested in the intersection between survey and spatial statistics and in the properties of areal aggregation incontinuous spatial models. I have also studied social aggregation and swarm behavior, inverse modeling of earthquake coseismic slips with paleoseismic data, climate feedback modeling, under-5 mortality estimation, and have worked on developing open source statistical software in R on both GitHub and CRAN. My my CV gives more information on my research, publications, presentations, and software.
