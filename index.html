About

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington. I specialize in spatial and spatiotemporal statistics, and am also interested in computational statistics, Bayesian statistics, INLA, small area estimation, and survey statistics. I am advised by Professors Jon Wakefield at UW, and Geir-Arne Fuglstad and Andrea Riebler at NTNU. I completed a B.A. with honors at Macalester College in Applied Mathematics and Statistics, minoring in Computer Science.
