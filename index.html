About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Statistics group in the Department of Mathematical Sciences Department of Mathematical Sciences at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU). I teach statistics courses at NTNU and also spend some of my time on research, studying small area estimation and accounting for positional error in spatial models with Jon Wakefield, Geir-Arne Fuglstad, and Andrea Riebler. In addition to my work on small area estimation and positional error, my research interests include Bayesian statistics, computational statistics, survey statistics, and integrated nested Laplace approximations (INLA).

Before coming to NTNU, I received my Ph.D. in Statistics at the University of Washington under Jon Wakefield, and my B.A. in Applied Math and Statistics at Macalester College.
